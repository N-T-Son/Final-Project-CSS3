About Bunny & Hare

Bunny & Hare is dedicated to showcasing the charm and diversity of rabbits and hares from around the globe. I will mostly sharing cute pciture of them!

They belong to a diverse group with over 60 species worldwide. Whether it's a tiny dwarf rabbit or a speedy jackrabbit, each has unique traits worth admiring.

Bunnies use their powerful hind legs not only for jumping but also to thump the ground as a warning signal to others.

Despite their cute appearance, hares are fierce survivors in the wild, adapted to escape threats with quick reflexes and camouflage.
